COCKTAIL RECIPES Written By: Cheah Jing Yi, Hong Yee Xuan, Wong Jun Sheng, Valen Tan, Yap Hau Yue Introduction Cocktail is an alcoholic mixed drink with a combination of one or more spirits mixed with other modifier ingredients such as syrup, fruit juice or creams. A cocktail can contain alcohol, sugar and bitters while some other mixed drinks contains only a distilled spirit and a mixer such as soda or fruit juice. It is normally prepared in a high ball. When a mixed drink contains distilled spirits and a liqueur, it is known as a duo but when it is added with a mixer it is known as a trio. Mixed drinks without alcohol are known as mocktails or virgin cocktails.
