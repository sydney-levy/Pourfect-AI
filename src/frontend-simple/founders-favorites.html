Founders' Favorites

  • Michelle's Dill-icous Pickleback

    Ingredients:

    • 4 oz Whiskey or Bourbon
    • 3 oz Pickle Juice
    • 1 ½ oz Fresh Lime Juice
    • 1 oz Simple Syrup
    • Pickle Spear and Fresh Dill to Garnish

    Instructions:

    1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice.
    2. Shake vigorously until well-chilled.
    3. Strain into a rocks glass filled with fresh ice.
    4. Garnish with a pickle spear and fresh dill.

  • Niki's Mocha Martini

    Ingredients:

    • 2 oz Vodka
    • 1 oz Coffee Liqueur
    • 1 oz Freshly Brewed Espresso
    • ½ oz Simple Syrup
    • Ice Cubes
    • Garnish: Coffee Beans

    Instructions:

    1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice.
    2. Shake vigorously to chill and froth.
    3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
    4. Garnish with three coffee beans.

  • Sydney's Sunrise Mule

    Ingredients:

    • 1.5 oz Vodka
    • 0.75 oz Fresh Lime Juice
    • 0.75 oz Passionfruit Pulp
    • Top with Ginger Beer
    • Garnish: Orange Wheel and Rosemary

    Instructions:

    1. Combine vodka, lime juice, and passionfruit pulp in a copper mug with ice.
    2. Top with ginger beer and stir gently.
    3. Garnish with an orange wheel and rosemary.

  • Aida's Sparkling Citrus Sprite

    Ingredients:

    • 4 oz Sprite
    • 2 oz Fresh Orange Juice
    • 1 oz Fresh Grapefruit Juice
    • ½ oz Fresh Lime Juice
    • 2 oz Soda Water
    • Garnish: Orange Slice and Lime Wedge

    Instructions:

    1. Combine Sprite, orange juice, grapefruit juice, and lime juice in a shaker with ice.
    2. Shake well to chill and mix flavors.
    3. Strain into a tall glass with ice.
    4. Top with soda water and garnish.

  • Soline's All-Nighter Express

    Ingredients:

    • 2 oz Dark Rum
    • 1 oz Fresh Pineapple Juice
    • 0.75 oz Lime Juice
    • 0.5 oz Agave Nectar
    • 2 Dashes Angostura Bitters
    • Top with Club Soda
    • Garnish: Pineapple Wedge and Cherry

    Instructions:

    1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice.
    2. Shake until chilled.
    3. Strain into a glass with ice.
    4. Top with club soda and garnish.
